Your favourite Desktop Pet

Desktop Alpaca is the virtual pet you need to brighten up your working days at the computer. Having a cute alpaca running around your desktop will make you happier and more productive. It hardly uses any memory and you can keep it active indefinitely. Get ready to love it, change its color, pick up its poop and move this fantastic animal all over your desktop. Don't like to be alone?

Today's jobs cause that we spend a lot of time in front of the computer. Being in front of the screen all day can be very lonely. Some people adopt pets to combat this loneliness, but having a physical pet is a lot of work. Why not enhance your life with a virtual alpaca? This desktop pet will give you love and companionship when you need it most.

TAKE CARE OF IT

Do you want the alpaca to love you? Then don't forget to double click on it to pet it. It is a very affectionate animal and loves to be loved.

CHOOSE YOUR COLOR

You can choose from more than 5 different colors for your alpaca! Using your mouse right click you can change the color of your alpaca. What color will you choose?

PICK UP ITS POOPS

No one said pet ownership was easy. The alpaca will poop as it walks across your desk and you will have to clean it with a click. But don't worry, it doesn't stain.
